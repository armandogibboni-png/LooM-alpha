# L.O.O.M. // SWARM CORE — v2.2 Alpha

**Local Opinion Overhaul Monitor**  
Browser-based intelligence network simulator. No install. No account. Free to play.

---

## What is this

You are an unnamed operative inserted into a classified network of 90 nodes distributed across 6 zones. Two Sovereigns control the flow of information inside the network. Your objective: neutralize both before suspicion burns your cover.

You do not fight. You observe, infiltrate, and dismantle influence — quietly, from the inside.

---

## How to play

**Deploy** into a zone to reveal assets. Nodes are encrypted until contact.  
**Interact** with featured assets to unlock dossiers and build trust.  
**Advance Turn** to progress the simulation — the network ticks, assets move, tension shifts.  
**Watch the log** — behavioral anomalies surface over time. One of the nodes is a mole.

### Neutralizing a Sovereign
Each Sovereign controls a faction. To neutralize one:
- Burn **4 of their followers** via PSYOP or DISINFO ops
- Or collect **3 secrets** via Intel Extract and Asset Search
- Both must be neutralized before **network suspicion reaches 95%**

### The Mole Hunt
Each turn, the mole generates behavioral clues in the intelligence log — anomalous zone changes, off-schedule access, unusual traffic patterns. When you have 2+ clues on an asset, you can **accuse them**. Correct: suspicion drops, +250 credits. Wrong: faction suspicion spikes and trust burns.

### Postures
| Posture | Effect |
|---|---|
| BASE | Remote ops only. Suspicion decays. |
| SHADOW | Invisible in zone. Costs 15 credits/turn. |
| STANDARD | Normal presence. Default risk. |
| EXPOSED | You are noticed. Trust builds faster. High risk. |

| Surveillance | 50 | Track asset 4 turns. |
| Room Bug | 70 | Reveals zone identities 3 turns. |
| PSYOP | 80 | Blacklist target. High suspicion cost. |

> Proximity bonus: ops cost **−20%** and are **×1.3 more effective** when you are in the same zone as the target.

## Technical

- **Engine**: local JavaScript — no server, no AI required to play
- **AI**: Groq API (optional) — narrative flavor + node conversations
- **Deploy**: Vercel — [loo-m-alpha.vercel.app](https://loo-m-alpha.vercel.app)
- **Stack**: Vanilla ES modules, SVG radar, no frameworks
